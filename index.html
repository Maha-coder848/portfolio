Dress E-Commerce Website Design

Designed and developed a fully responsive, user-friendly e-commerce website for an online dress store. The website is designed to provide an engaging and seamless shopping experience across all devices, with an intuitive layout and a fully functional shopping cart system for an effortless purchase journey.

Technologies Used:

HTML5
CSS3 (including Flexbox and Grid for layout)
JavaScript for dynamic functionalities (e.g., shopping cart, product filters)
Responsive Web Design to ensure the website adapts to all screen sizes, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops
Key Features:

Responsive Design: The website is optimized for all device types, ensuring users enjoy a consistent experience whether they’re on a phone, tablet, or desktop.
Product Showcases: High-resolution images of dresses with zoom-in functionality, detailed descriptions, and customer reviews to help users make informed purchasing decisions.
Intuitive Navigation: Easy-to-use navigation menu and search filters for quick product discovery by category, size, color, and brand.
Dynamic Shopping Cart & Checkout:
Add/Remove Products: Users can easily add or remove items from the shopping cart in real-time.
Quantity Adjustment: Allows users to update the quantity of each item directly within the cart.
Cart Summary: The cart displays product details, quantities, individual prices, and total cost dynamically.
Secure Checkout: Features a smooth, secure checkout process with multiple payment options, including guest checkout.
Interactive Elements: Dynamic filters, product comparisons, and hover effects enhance the interactivity and engagement of the site. Blog Section: A blog featuring style tips, new arrivals, and fashion trends to keep users engaged and encourage return visits.
